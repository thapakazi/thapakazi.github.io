Day1

  • Two major sources: bhojan and sakaratmak sochan

  • 4 major form of energy:

    • physical energy{भौतिक शक्ति}{food, timing, exercieseब्यायाम, प्राणायाम}
    • mental energy{बिरचार-मनकको शक्ति}
    • emotional energy{भाव बाट उत्पन्न हुने शक्ति}
    • spiritual energy{अाध्यात्मिक शक्ति}

    The value of them is such

    • physical energy < mental energy by 125 to 1048 times
    • mental energy < emotional energy by 1048 times
    • emotional energy < spiritual energy by thousand of times, immeasurable

    But all of those are also like four major pillars of energy form inside us. They all are needed in a human life.

  • Karma: 3type {self, group, natural}

  • Urja Bigyan, focus is on:

    • working your biggest problems with basis of energy
    • aspiring sakaratmak swoch (+ve thinking) to deal with existing problems
    • Kriya(steps) to empower the hidden energy
    • primarily working with Sahasrara Chakra

Energy yet another prospective

OXYZEN is energy that drives us, our brain, our heart, process in every cells. We intake oxyzen in 3 forms solid, liquid & gas. Ways we can enhance our oxyzen intake:

  • gas:
    • long-and-deep breath
    • arrangements to get fresh air (early 30 mins walk with morning sun)
  • liquid:
    • taking water, 4ltrs a day
  • solid:
    • fresh foods {stale food; only eat if must}
    • green vegetables {हरियो परियो }
    • fruits{पोटिलो अानि रसिलो}
